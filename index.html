About the Department

The Department of Microbiology provides comprehensive microbiological diagnostic services, supporting clinicians with accurate pathogen identification and antibiotic sensitivity testing.

We operate BSL-2 certified facilities covering bacteriology, virology, mycology, parasitology, and molecular diagnostics — available 24 hours a day.
